Psycho Predictor

Utilizing Machine Learning to Understand and Predict Mental Health Needs in the Tech Workplace.

About The Project

This application leverages a powerful Random Forest machine learning model to analyze various factors related to the work environment and personal history. Based on the "Mental Health in Tech Survey" dataset, it predicts the likelihood of an individual seeking treatment for a mental health condition.

How It Works

The process is simple yet powerful:

  1. Input Data: You provide anonymous information through the form on the right, covering demographics, work environment, and attitudes towards mental health.
  2. Live Prediction: Our trained model processes this information in real-time.
  3. Get Results: The model outputs a prediction indicating the likelihood of seeking treatment, along with a confidence score.

Important Disclaimer

This is not a medical diagnosis. This tool is an educational project and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are experiencing mental health challenges, please consult a qualified healthcare provider.
